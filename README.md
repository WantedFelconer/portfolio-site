# WantedFelconer Portfolio

This is my personal portfolio website, designed with a **dark Material UI-inspired layout**. I built it to showcase who I am, my featured projects, and my social presence in a clean, layered, and intuitive interface.

---

## ✨ What I Built

- **Dark Material UI Layout**  
  I chose a sleek, professional dark theme with indigo accents, layered sections, and elevated cards to give the site depth and polish.

- **Hero Section**  
  My circular avatar sits beside my name *WantedFelconer*. Underneath, I highlight my designation:  
  *Backend Integration Specialist | Brac University CS Student*.

- **Intro Video**  
  At the center of the page, I embedded my YouTube intro video so visitors can get a quick sense of me.

- **Featured Projects**  
  I placed my projects at the center of the page, displayed in elevated cards with hover animations.

- **Animated Social Icons**  
  At the bottom, I added icons for Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, X, YouTube, and GitHub. They animate when hovered and open my profiles in new tabs.

- **Sliding Sidebar Menu**  
  I implemented a sidebar menu that slides in when the menu icon is clicked. Inside, my social links are displayed in tile format with hover effects.

## 🚀 How I Deployed It

I used **GitHub Pages** to host this portfolio:

1. I pushed the project to my GitHub repository.
2. In **Settings → Pages**, I selected branch `main` and root `/`.
3. My site is now live at:
   ```
   https://wantedfelconer.github.io/portfolio/
   ```
